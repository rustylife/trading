Self-reflection questions from Jason Shapiro on how to read the market consensus:
1. Are we at peak fear or peak greed? What do people say? What do people anticipate is going to happen?
Pay attention to the keywords: despite, in spite of, nonetheless, nevertheless, even so, etc.
2. Are you yourself fearful or greedy? What do you think is going to happen?
3. What's the scariest trade to execute for you right now?
4. Is it easier for you to go long or short here?
5. What would be the market move that would surprise everybody?
6. What would surprise you, personally?
7. Where do you believe the market is going from here? Are you waiting to buy or sell?
8. How are you positioned?
9. How are people positioned per CFTC COT report and actively managed ETFs?
