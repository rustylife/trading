Self-reflection questions from Jason Shapiro on how to read the market consensus:
1. Are we at peak fear or peak greed? What to people say? Pay attention to the keywords: despite, in spite of, nonetheless, nevertheless, even so, etc.
2. Are you yourself fearful or greedy?
3. What's the scariest trade to execute for you right now?
4. Is it easier for you to go long or short here?
5. What would be the market move that would surprise everybody?
6. Where do you believe the market is going from here?
7. What would surprise you, personally?
8. How are you positioned?
9. How are people positioned per CFTC COT report?
